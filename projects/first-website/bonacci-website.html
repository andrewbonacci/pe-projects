The Llanerch Diner

The Llanerch Diner is a restaurant in Philadelphia, PA. It is my favorite restaurant because of its easy-going atmosphere. I also like the fact that the diner is always open, so even if you want to go in for a little snack, you can do so at any time. In short, I love this place because:

  • The food is delicious
  • The staff are friendly
  • It's open 24/7

Types of Food

The Llanerch Diner serves a variety of food. You can enjoy anything from burgers to wraps or even full course meals. If you have a sweet tooth, desserts are plenty!

BURGERS

The most used ingredients in the burgers here are:

  1. All-beef patties
  2. Lettuce
  3. Red onions

WRAPS

The most used ingredients in the wraps here are:

  1. Spinach tortillas
  2. Grilled chicken
  3. American cheese
